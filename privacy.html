Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Perdypto handles data while providing AI-generated predictions.

Information We Collect

Perdypto does not collect personal data from users. We do not provide user accounts, profile systems, identity collection, behavioral tracking or personal analytics. You can view the platform without submitting your name, email address, wallet address or other personal information.

API Calls

When you request coin predictions, those requests are sent to our backend services so the platform can fetch market data, analyze news, run technical analysis and generate prediction output. These requests identify the coin being requested, but no personal information is attached by the frontend.

Cookies

Perdypto does not use cookies or local storage for tracking. We do not store tracking identifiers in your browser and we do not use browser storage to build user profiles.

Third Party Services

Perdypto uses third party services to operate its prediction pipeline. Tavily may be used for news search, NVIDIA NIM may be used for AI reasoning, and Binance or OKX may be used for price data. These services process the operational request data needed to provide market summaries and predictions.

Data Retention

Predictions are cached for 15 minutes to improve performance and reduce repeated service calls. After the cache period expires, cached predictions are treated as expired and may be replaced or removed. Perdypto does not retain personal user profiles because the platform does not collect personal user data.

Children's Privacy

Perdypto is not intended for people under 18. The platform discusses cryptocurrency markets, trading risk and financial uncertainty, and should only be used by adults who can understand those risks.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the platform, services or operational practices. Continued use of Perdypto after an updated policy is posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions contact us through our platform.
